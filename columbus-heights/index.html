Columbus Heights. since 1985

High quality accommodation services

Columbus Heights in Ocho Rios is your own private luxury hillside condo overlooking the beautiful Bay and panoramic city views. Centrally located studios, one and two bedrooms furnished apartments with kitchen, living room, private balcony and daily maid service make this a dream come true.
Large complex featuring full kitchen, living room, private balcony, safe & daily maid service * 5 oversized swimming pools * Daily shuttle bus to Ocho Rios * Watersports nearby.
A ten minute drive to Dunn's River falls, Fern Gully, Shaw Park, and most of the major attractions. The beach is at the bottom of the hill with diving, jet ski, parasailing, helicopter tours, fishing and every manner of water sports.
A five minute walk to the bottom of hill and you are in Island Village. This is one of the newest and hottest places in Jamaica. The beach is fantastic and features a large free waterslide.
